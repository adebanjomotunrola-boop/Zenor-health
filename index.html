About Our HMO

Zenor Healthcare ltd is a Health maintenance organsation (HMO) duly registerd with the CAC to operate as an HMO in Nigeria. We are committed to providing accessible, affordable, and quality healthcare services through a robust network of hospitals, clinics, and specialists. Our goal is to prioritize your health and well-being every step of the way.

Exclusions

The following services and conditions are not covered under your HMO health plan.

  • Cosmetic or aesthetic treatments
  • Infertility diagnosis or treatments
  • Experimental or non-approved therapies
  • Organ transplants not included in policy
  • Non-prescription drugs and supplements
  • Injuries from illegal activities or self-harm
  • Overseas treatments not pre-approved
